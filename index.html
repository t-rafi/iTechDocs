Introduction

Thank you for choosing iTech Velocity. This documentation will guide you through the structure, reports, and usage of the system. Our goal is to provide a clean, maintainable, and scalable solution for enterprise reporting needs.

Project Structure

After extracting the package, you will find:

  • assets/ – CSS, JS, images, fonts
  • partials/ – Header, Sidebar, Footer
  • index.html – Introduction page
  • report.html – Reports listing
  • reports.json – Centralized report data
